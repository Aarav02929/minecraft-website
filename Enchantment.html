Minecraft Enchantment Guide

How to Craft an Enchanting Table:

To craft an enchanting table, you need:

  • 1 book
  • 2 diamonds
  • 4 obsidian

Crafting Recipe:

  1. Place 1 book in the top middle slot.
  2. Place 2 diamonds on either side of the middle row.
  3. Place 4 obsidian in an upside-down "T" shape (3 along the bottom row and 1 in the center).

How to Use the Enchanting Table:

Once you place your enchanting table down, you need lapis lazuli and experience levels (XP) to enchant items.

  1. Place the item you want to enchant in the table's left slot.
  2. Add lapis lazuli in the right slot.
  3. Choose from the three available enchantments based on your XP level.

Boosting Enchantments with Bookshelves:

Surrounding the enchanting table with bookshelves increases the level of enchantments available. Place bookshelves one block away from the enchanting table (on the same level or one block above). You'll need 15 bookshelves for the maximum enchantment level of 30.

Items You Can Enchant:

  • Weapons (swords, bows, crossbows, tridents)
  • Tools (pickaxes, axes, shovels, hoes, fishing rods)
  • Armor (helmets, chestplates, leggings, boots, shields)
  • Books (for storing enchantments)

Types of Enchantments:

  • Sharpness (increases damage for swords and axes)
  • Efficiency (increases mining speed for tools)
  • Protection (reduces damage for armor)
  • Unbreaking (increases item durability)
  • Fortune (increases drops from mining)
  • Power (increases bow damage)
  • Mending (repairs item durability using XP)
  • Silk Touch (collect blocks in their original form)

Anvil and Enchanted Books:

Using an anvil, you can combine enchantments or apply them from enchanted books, as well as repair items and combine enchantments. You can find enchanted books in treasure chests, through trading with villagers, or by fishing.
